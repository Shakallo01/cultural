About Our Association

Miyyeti Allah Kautal Hore is the foremost Fulani socio-cultural organization in Nigeria, dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage, values, and traditions of the Fulani people.

Established with the vision of fostering unity, peace, and development among the Fulani community and Nigeria at large, our association works tirelessly to educate, empower, and engage both members and the wider public.

Under the distinguished leadership of our National President and the dedicated efforts of our National Director of Culture, we strive to showcase the positive contributions of the Fulani people to Nigeria's socio-cultural tapestry.

Fulani Cultural Values

Pulaaku

The Fulani code of conduct emphasizing patience, self-control, discipline, prudence, and respect for others.

Community

Strong community bonds and mutual support systems that have sustained Fulani society for centuries.

Education

Commitment to knowledge acquisition and preservation, both Islamic and traditional.

Peaceful Coexistence

Promoting harmony and understanding between all ethnic groups in Nigeria.
