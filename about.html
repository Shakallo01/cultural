About Our Association

Our Mission

To preserve, promote, and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of the Fulani people while fostering unity, peace, and development within the Fulani community and throughout Nigeria.

Our Vision

To be the foremost organization championing the positive contributions of the Fulani people to Nigeria's socio-cultural tapestry while promoting harmonious coexistence with all ethnic groups.

Our History

Miyyeti Allah Kautal Hore was established with the vision of creating a unified platform for the Fulani people across Nigeria. Our association emerged from the need to preserve our cultural identity while contributing positively to national development.

Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to educate, empower, and engage both members of the Fulani community and the wider public about our rich heritage, values, and traditions.

Under the distinguished leadership of our National President and the dedicated efforts of our National Director of Culture, we have grown to become the premier Fulani socio-cultural organization in Nigeria.

Our Objectives

Cultural Preservation

Document, preserve, and promote Fulani cultural heritage, including language, traditions, music, and pastoral practices.

Community Development

Initiate and support development projects that improve the lives of Fulani communities across Nigeria.

Peace Building

Promote peaceful coexistence and understanding between Fulani communities and other ethnic groups in Nigeria.

Education Advocacy

Advocate for educational opportunities and create scholarship programs for Fulani youth.

Organizational Structure

Miyyeti Allah Kautal Hore operates through a well-defined structure that ensures effective governance and representation at all levels:

National Executive Council

The highest decision-making body, comprising elected representatives from all states and zones.

Zonal Chapters

Zonal-level structures that implement national programs and address State issues.

State Chapters

State-level structures that implement national programs and address local issues.

Local Government Coordinators

Grassroots representatives who ensure community participation and program implementation.

Special Committees

Task-specific committees focusing on areas such as education, conflict resolution, and cultural affairs.
